# The Digital Economist

<p align="center">
  <strong>Building a human-centered digital economy—connecting disciplines, systems, and innovation to serve people and the planet.</strong>
</p>

At **The Digital Economist**, we curate interdisciplinary research, strategic insights, and policy-driven thought leadership to address the defining challenges of our time. Our work bridges technology, economics, governance, and sustainability to create practical solutions for a more inclusive, resilient, and future-ready world.

---

## 🌍 Our Research Themes

Our research is organized into dedicated workgroups, each exploring a critical area shaping the future of the digital economy.

- ### 🤖 Applied Artificial Intelligence
    Investigating the applications, ethics, governance, and societal impact of AI to foster responsible and inclusive innovation.

- ### ⛓️ Digital Assets & Blockchain
    Exploring decentralized technologies and digital assets that are transforming institutions, financial systems, and trust in the digital age.

- ### 🏛️ Tech Policy & Governance
    Developing policy frameworks and governance models that enable transparent, participatory, and resilient digital societies.

- ### 🌱 Sustainability in Tech
    Examining how technology can accelerate sustainable development and the transition toward regenerative economic systems.

- ### 🌐 Regenerative Digital Infrastructure
    Researching next-generation digital infrastructure that is resilient, inclusive, and designed for long-term societal value.

- ### ⚛️ Quantum Computing
    Understanding emerging quantum technologies and their implications for science, cybersecurity, and industry.

- ### 🏥 Healthcare Innovation
    Exploring the intersection of healthcare, technology, and equity to build accessible and digitally enabled health systems.

- ### 🛡️ Cybersecurity
    Studying digital security, cyber resilience, privacy, and trust in increasingly connected societies.

## 📚 Our Research Repositories

Our public repositories serve as a growing collection of research papers, publications, and knowledge produced by **The Digital Economist Fellows**.

Each repository represents a dedicated research workgroup, where fellows contribute original research, technical analyses, policy papers, and thought leadership based on their area of expertise.

Current public research repositories include:

- 🤖 Applied Artificial Intelligence

- 🌱 Sustainability in Tech

- ⚛️ Quantum Computing

These repositories are continuously updated as our fellows publish new work, collaborate across disciplines, and contribute to advancing the digital economy through open research.

## 🤝 Get Involved

We actively welcome collaboration with researchers, policymakers, technologists, students, and ecosystem leaders who are passionate about shaping the future of technology and society.

If you'd like to contribute:

1. Fork the relevant repository.
2. Create a feature branch.

We value contributions of all kinds—from research and documentation to software development, data analysis, and new ideas.
